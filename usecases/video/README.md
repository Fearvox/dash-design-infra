# Video Use Cases

Public-safe workflow notes for rendered motion pieces. These are not raw lab dumps; they keep the reusable production discipline around render, QA, compression, and delivery.

| Usecase | Good for |
|---|---|
| [Windburn Render Workflow](./windburn-render-workflow.md) | chunked generative video render, contact-sheet QA, and bitrate-controlled WeChat delivery |

## Boundary

Source videos, private audio, local render paths, and final project media stay outside this repository. The docs keep the part that can be reused safely: the contract, checks, commands, and failure modes.
